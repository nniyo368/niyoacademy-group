Murakaza neza kuri Niyo Academy Group

Tugufasha kubona ibibazo,ibitabo,ndetse na notes n'ibizamini bya leta bigufasha gusubiramo amasomo yawe no kukwagurira uburyo bw'imitekerereze.

Programs & Subjects (National Exams)

Note: Ibizamini bya Leta bibera ku rwego rwa P6, S3 (O-Level), na S6 (A-Level). Andi masomo(P1–P5, S1–S2, S4–S5) ni ibizamini by’ishuri (school-based).
PRIMARY (P1 – P6) ▼

P1 – P5 School-based

Amasomo rusange yigishwa:

  • Kinyarwanda, English
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Elementary Technology (SET)
  • Social Studies & Religious Education (SRE)
  • ICT, Creative Arts, Physical Education

P6 – National Examination

Amasomo akorwa mu bizamini bya Leta (P6):

  • Kinyarwanda
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Elementary Technology (SET)
  • Social Studies & Religious Education (SRE)
O-LEVEL (S1 – S3) ▼

S1 – S2 School-based

  • English, Kinyarwanda
  • Mathematics
  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • History, Geography
  • Entrepreneurship, ICT
  • French/Kiswahili (depending on school)

S3 – National Examination

Amasomo asanzwe akorerwamo ibizamini bya Leta (ashobora gutandukana bitewe n’amasomo wahisemo):

  • English, Kinyarwanda
  • Mathematics
  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • History, Geography
  • Entrepreneurship
  • French cyangwa Kiswahili (nk’iyinjizwa)
A-LEVEL (S4 – S6) ▼

S4 – S5 By combinations (School-based)

Uhitamo combination (urugero): PCB, MCB, PCM, HEG, HGL, LFK, MEG, n’izindi.

S6 – National Examination

Ibizamini bya Leta bikorwa hashingiwe kuri combination wahisemo. Urugero:

  • Science combinations (urugero PCB/MCB/PCM): Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, (Biology/Computer Science bitewe n’iyo wahisemo)
  • Humanities/Economics (HEG/HGL/MEG…): History, Geography, Economics, Literature in English/Kinyarwanda/Kiswahili/French (bitewe n’iyatoranyijwe)
  • Languages/Literature (LFK…): Literature in English, French, Kinyarwanda/Kiswahili, n’ayandi y’iyongere

TVET tracks zifite amasomo yihariye ajyana n’umwuga (zishobora gutandukana n’aya hejuru).
